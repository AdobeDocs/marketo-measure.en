# Stock Reports and Dashboards in Dynamics {#stock-reports-and-dashboards-in-dynamics}

>[!NOTE]
>
>You may see instructions specifying "[!DNL Marketo Measure]" in the documentation, but still see "Bizible" in your CRM. We are working to have that updated and the rebranding will be reflected in your CRM soon.

The [!DNL Marketo Measure] Managed Solution comes with several stock Dashboards and Reports, designed to cover your basic needs when viewing [!DNL Marketo Measure] data in Dynamics. Below is the full list and some common use cases.

## Dashboards (nine total) {#dashboards-total}

| Dashboard Name | Use Case | Billing Group |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] CMO Dashboard with First Touch | As a CMO, view the current health of your marketing performance from charts such as revenue by marketing channel, top sources, and revenue over time. This dashboard reports on the first touch of an opportunity's journey. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] CMO Dashboard with Lead Creation Touch | As a CMO, view the current health of your marketing performance from charts such as revenue by marketing channel, top sources, and revenue over time. This dashboard reports on the lead creation touch of an opportunity's journey. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] CMO Dashboard with Multi Touch | As a CMO, view the current health of your marketing performance from charts such as revenue by marketing channel, top sources, and revenue over time. This dashboard reports on all key touches of an opportunity's journey with W-Shaped weighting. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] Content Marketing Dashboard with First Touch | As a Content Marketer, view the current health of your content from charts such as leads generated by landing page, source, and form URL. This dashboard reports on the first touch of a leads journey. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] Content Marketing Dashboard with Lead Creation Touch | As a Content Marketer, view the current health of your content from charts such as leads generated by landing page, source, and form URL. This dashboard reports on the lead creation touch of a leads journey. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] Content Marketing Dashboard with Multi Touch | As a Content Marketer, view the current health of your content from charts such as leads generated by landing page, source, and form URL. This dashboard reports on all key touches of a leads journey with W-Shaped weighting. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] Paid Media Dashboard with First Touch | As a Paid Media Manager, view the current health of your paid media from charts such as leads generated by source, campaign name, or search keyword. This dashboard reports on the first touch of a leads journey. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] Paid Media Dashboard with Lead Creation Touch | As a Paid Media Manager, view the current health of your paid media from charts such as leads generated by source, campaign name, or search keyword. This dashboard reports on the lead creation touch of a leads journey. | All |
| [!DNL Marketo Measure] Paid Media Dashboard with Multi Touch | As a Paid Media Manager, view the current health of your paid media from charts such as leads generated by source, campaign name, or search keyword.
